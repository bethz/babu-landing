BABU

Be A Better yoU! ✨
Prototype by BethZ. This is an experimental communication coaching skill. Nothing is guaranteed. Results depend on transcript availability, meeting context, and the inherent limitations of AI-based analysis. Use it as a growth tool, not a performance metric.

Please do not share this repo directly. If someone is interested, have them reach out to me for access. The goal is to grow this together as a community, not distribute an unfinished tool.

A communication coaching skill for GitHub Copilot CLI that evaluates your speaking patterns, executive presence, and behavioral habits from meeting transcripts.

BABU pulls your actual words from Teams meeting transcripts, scores them against a structured framework, and gives you specific, actionable coaching, the same way a live executive coach would.

What It Does

  • Analyzes your verbatim quotes from meeting transcripts (via Microsoft 365 Copilot / WorkIQ)
  • Scores you across 9 categories with a three-tier rating system
  • Generates a before/after reframe table: what you said vs. what you could say
  • Tracks progress over time with report cards and trend arrows
  • Scans for 13 behavioral patterns that damage trust (interrupting, dismissiveness, passive aggression, etc.)
  • Evaluates executive presence: gravitas, communication, engagement
  • Compares your voice to CEO vs. CTO communication styles
  • Delivers feedback with coaching warmth, not criticism

Categories Evaluated

#CategoryWhat It Measures
1Power LanguageHedge words vs. commitment language
2SpecificityVague references vs. precise names, dates, numbers
3Tense OrientationBackward-looking ("what I did") vs. forward-looking ("what this enables")
4Speaking DynamicsTalk time, first contribution timing, floor recovery, filler density
5Influence & FramingAnchoring, attribution, storytelling structure, closing strength
6Executive PresenceGravitas, communication clarity, engagement in group settings
7CEO vs. CTO VoiceWhich executive voice dominates, and does it match the room
8Audience CalibrationVocabulary shifting across different meeting types
9Behavioral Awareness13-behavior scan for trust-damaging patterns

Installation

Copy the SKILL.md file to your Copilot CLI skills directory:

# macOS / Linux
mkdir -p ~/.copilot/skills/babu
cp SKILL.md ~/.copilot/skills/babu/

# Windows (PowerShell)
New-Item -ItemType Directory -Path "$env:USERPROFILE\.copilot\skills\babu" -Force
Copy-Item SKILL.md "$env:USERPROFILE\.copilot\skills\babu\"

Start a new Copilot CLI session. BABU will be available automatically.

Requirements

  • GitHub Copilot CLI
  • Microsoft 365 account with Teams meeting transcripts enabled
  • WorkIQ plugin (for pulling meeting transcript data)

Usage

Just ask naturally. BABU activates when you ask about communication, speaking, or meeting performance:

  • "How do I sound in meetings this week?"
  • "Evaluate my executive presence"
  • "How do I sound with [person name]?"
  • "Give me a BABU report"
  • "What bad habits do I have in meetings?"
  • "Am I improving?"

Every report includes:

  1. A warm welcome and positive reinforcement
  2. Report card with scores and trends
  3. Wins to celebrate
  4. A reframe table (what you say vs. what you could say)
  5. Behavioral check (13 behaviors scanned)
  6. Executive presence scorecard
  7. CEO/CTO voice analysis
  8. Practice assignments for the week
  9. Coach's closing note

Philosophy

BABU is a coach, not a critic.

  • Leads with wins before growth areas
  • Frames gaps as "next level," not "problems"
  • Grounds every positive statement in transcript evidence (no empty flattery)
  • Normalizes the journey (everyone has patterns)
  • Gives permission to be imperfect
  • Ends every session with momentum
